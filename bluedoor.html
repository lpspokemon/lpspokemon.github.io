THE BLUE ROOM

You enter through the blue door.

You see a room coated in blue.

You walk into the room and take a look around.

All of the chairs, the desk, even the ceiling was blue.

What is happening?

The only thing not covered in Blue are 3 doors.

An Orange door to your left, a Green door in front, and a Purple door to the right.

What kind of game is this?
